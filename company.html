Our values

Better Together

We prioritize people – both in our products and our workplace culture. We foster a collaborative and ego-free environment all while having fun!

Generosity

We feel privileged to be in this space and want to give back as much as we can out of love for the dental industry.

Open Door Policy

We celebrate open and honest conversations, encouraging transparency with our team and partners. This keeps everyone on the same page and ensures they understand that we’re all chasing the same goals.

Perseverance

Perseverance is in our founder’s DNA. We believe growth comes from action. The more we do, the closer we get to our goals.

Dr. Claude
Chamoun, DDS

Founder, Inventor and Chief Clinical Officer

Practicing for over 18 years, Dr. Chamoun’s passion for his profession drove him to wanting more. He believes that enhancing the overall experience in a dental practice, for both dental practitioners and patients, will greatly benefit the industry. The Halo Digital Mirror was born out of necessity, passion and ambition. While still practicing, he built a team to bring his idea to life. Now 3 years later, he’s excited to share the Halo Digital Mirror with the world.
